# Fuad Taufiqul Hakim
## Biotechnologist and Programmer

I'm Fuad, a biotechnologist and self-taught programmer from Bangladesh. I completed my B. Sc. and M. Sc. in Biotechnology from the University of Rajshahi. I learned programming to apply computation to the biotech sector for extensive study.

I have decided to pursue a Ph.D. in the field of biosciences. I would particularly like to study the biochemical mechanisms of immunity and microbial modes of action, thereby designing and developing Nobel drugs by applying computation. My long-term career goal is to establish an independent research laboratory in Bangladesh.
